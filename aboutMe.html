More Than a T Shape Designer

I am inherently a collaborative as a designer. My Journalism background enables me to commmunicate and share people's stories with different kinds of audiences. My experience as a Business woman helps me speak the language of business leaders and Fiscal Year KPIs when making design decisions.

With all this, I specialize in Product Design and Problem Solving. I'm excited by the process of deriving user needs from research, creatively leveraging the power of technology to satisfy those needs, and thinking critically about the contstraints and edge cases to maek sure that the solution scales.



Intentional



Everything is a conversation. I see all interactions are relationships with the user. There should be a reason behind every design decision.



We follow the double dimond design process - but it's rarely every that smooth. The real process is full of many iterations and consistent questioning of my own design.

My philosphy of designing is to always experiment with different approaches, to fail and pivot fast, and to consistently be open to feedback and ways that you can improve the way you look at design and other designs



TechHead



Born and raised in the Bay Area with great zeal for exploring the world, I have been crafting digital experiences in the United States since I used the first generation Mac computer.

Perhaps being born in the Silicon Valley and drinking the water - it keeps me excited by the latest technologies and their potential. I spend my extra time getting into Augmented Reality experience design because I'm intrigued by the capability of how it can improve human life from personal to professional lives.

As a self-motivated individual, I keep pursuing cutting-edge design techniques while reflecting on the fundamental human nature.
